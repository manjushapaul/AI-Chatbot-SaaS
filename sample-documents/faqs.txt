FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

GENERAL QUESTIONS

Q: What is an AI chatbot?
A: An AI chatbot is a computer program that uses artificial intelligence to simulate human conversation. It can understand user questions and provide relevant, helpful responses based on its training data.

Q: How does the AI chatbot learn?
A: Our AI chatbots learn through machine learning algorithms that process the knowledge base documents you upload, conversations they have with users, and feedback you provide. They continuously improve over time.

Q: What types of businesses can use this platform?
A: Any business that wants to provide 24/7 customer support, answer common questions, or automate customer interactions can benefit from our AI chatbot platform. This includes e-commerce, healthcare, education, and service industries.

TECHNICAL QUESTIONS

Q: What file formats are supported for document uploads?
A: We support PDF, Word (.docx), Text (.txt), HTML, Markdown (.md), and JSON files. All documents are automatically processed and optimized for AI training.

Q: How secure is my data?
A: We use enterprise-grade security measures including encryption, secure data centers, and strict access controls. Your data is never shared with third parties and is protected by industry-standard security protocols.

Q: Can I integrate the chatbot with my existing systems?
A: Yes! We provide API access, webhook support, and integrations with popular platforms like Slack, Teams, and Discord. You can also embed the chatbot directly on your website.

Q: What AI models are available?
A: We currently support GPT-3.5, GPT-4, and Claude models. Each has different strengths in terms of speed, accuracy, and cost. You can choose the best model for your specific use case.
