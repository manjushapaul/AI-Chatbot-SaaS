COMPANY POLICIES AND PROCEDURES

1. WORKPLACE CONDUCT
   - All employees must maintain professional behavior
   - Respect for colleagues and customers is mandatory
   - No harassment or discrimination will be tolerated
   - Dress code: Business casual attire required

2. WORKING HOURS
   - Standard work week: Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
   - Flexible hours available with manager approval
   - Overtime must be pre-approved
   - Lunch break: 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM

3. LEAVE POLICIES
   - Vacation: 20 days per year
   - Sick leave: 10 days per year
   - Personal days: 5 days per year
   - All leave requests require 2 weeks notice

4. REMOTE WORK
   - Hybrid work model available
   - Remote work requires stable internet connection
   - Core hours: 10:00 AM - 3:00 PM must be available
   - Regular check-ins with team required

5. EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT
   - Submit expense reports within 30 days
   - Receipts required for all expenses over $25
   - Travel expenses require pre-approval
   - Maximum daily meal allowance: $50

6. IT SECURITY
   - Strong passwords required (minimum 8 characters)
   - Two-factor authentication mandatory
   - No personal devices for work without approval
   - Regular security training required

7. PERFORMANCE REVIEWS
   - Annual performance reviews in December
   - Quarterly check-ins with managers
   - Goals set at beginning of each year
   - Feedback sessions available upon request

8. TRAINING AND DEVELOPMENT
   - Annual training budget: $1,000 per employee
   - Conference attendance encouraged
   - Online learning platforms available
   - Mentorship program available

9. HEALTH AND SAFETY
   - Regular workplace safety inspections
   - Emergency procedures posted in all areas
   - First aid kits available on each floor
   - Report all incidents immediately

10. CONFIDENTIALITY
    - All company information is confidential
    - Non-disclosure agreements required
    - Social media posts about work prohibited
    - Client information strictly protected

For questions about any policy, contact HR department or your immediate supervisor.
